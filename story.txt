0.-The Protagonist arrives at the army without much motivation
1.- The Protagonist is assigned a dog as a partner and he is in denial about that.
2.- As they train they start to get along with each other and slowly become friends.
3.- The Sergeant of the army entrusts The Protagonist with a very dangerous mission
that will help to end the war. Because of the threat of the mission, The Protagonist 
decides to leave behind his dog partner.
4.- As the mission develops The Protagonist gets captured.
5.- When all hope is lost for The Protagonist, his dog partner arrives and frees him.
They both, complete the mission but unfortunately they die as they do it.
6.- Thanks to their service, the war ends and they get immortalized in a statue.
